Verteil Technologies awarded Destination Kerala’s Startup Award 2017

Company Update
Feb 17, 2018

In a glittering event on 16 February 2018 at Hotel Crowne Plaza, Kochi, India , Verteil Technologies was awarded Destination Kerala’s Startup Award 2017 for innovations in the civil aviation sector selected by an imminent jury panel constituted from KSIDC & KSUM, Govt of Kerala, India and TiE-Kerala Chapter.
