Verteil Technologies launches American Airlines NDC channel in India

Verteil Press Release
April 20, 2018

Verteil Technologies is thrilled to announce American Airlines NDC channel going live in India on our platform Verteil Direct Connect. On April 18th 2018, one of our partner agencies, Jose Travels, an IATA accredited travel agent based out of Indore, India issued three tickets between Seattle and New York directly connecting to the airline using the Verteil Direct Connect (VDC) technology platform and thereby becoming the first travel agency in India to take advantage of the offers coming directly from American Airlines.

After our successful launch of NDC in India last year, it gives me tremendous joy in bringing our first north american airline, American Airlines to our direct connect platform, Verteil Direct Connect.

Jerrin Jos

American Airlines embraces NDC.

“"After our successful launch of NDC in India last year, it gives me tremendous joy in bringing our first north american airline, American Airlines to our direct connect platform, Verteil Direct Connect. We are looking forward to working closely with American Airlines in opening up AA’s content directly to all our travel service provider partners in India and Asia Pacific regions” said Jerrin Jos, Founder & CEO, Verteil Technologies.

It is wonderful to know that Jose Travels is the first IATA accredited agency in India to have issued the first set of tickets on American Airlines’ NDC channel powered by Verteil Direct Connect.

T.K. Jose, Founder, Jose Travels

“It is wonderful to know that Jose Travels is the first IATA accredited agency in India to have issued the first set of tickets on American Airlines’ NDC channel powered by Verteil Direct Connect. The shopping, booking and ticketing process on Verteil Direct Connect is simple and easy to use like any modern e-commerce platform. Looking forward to working with Verteil for years to come” said T.K. Jose, Founder, Jose Travels.
