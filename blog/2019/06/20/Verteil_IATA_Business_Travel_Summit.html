Company Update
June 19-20, 2019

Verteil Technologies was invited by IATA to attend this year’s IATA Business Travel Summit in Geneva to showcase a live case study of how Wipro Travel Services, Bangalore is using our NDC based flagship product, Verteil Direct Connect to manage the business travel needs of Wipro IT employees worldwide.

Verteil was represented by Satheesh Satchit, Co-Founder and Prajoth Kumar, VP of Business Development. Our airline partner Lufthansa represented by Jose Pereira also joined Verteil on stage for the demonstration. It was a good opportunity to interact with the business travel community and learn about the challenges the TMC’s face in facilitating the business travel requirements of their clients. We are confident that NDC with all its inherent features along with our airline partners will bring about a major change by bringing more transparency to their NDC offer and provide relevant offers at the right time for the business traveller.
